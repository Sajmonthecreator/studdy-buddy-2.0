Our Mission

To make quality study support accessible to every student, everywhere, through personalised online connections.

Our Background

StudyBuddy Connect was founded in 2023 by a group of former educators and university students who saw a gap in traditional tutoring models. We realized that many students primarily needed help organizing their study time, understanding homework assignments, and preparing for exams efficiently.

Since then, we have connected over 10,000 students with mentors from top universities around the globe. We believe that learning is better when we do it together.
